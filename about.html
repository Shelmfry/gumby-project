Meet Audrey

I make really, really good toffee.

I've had a sweet tooth since I was born. I think I've finally settled on my favorite treat, though - toffee. It's scientifically proven to be the best dessert. Okay, that's not true, but someday someone's going to fund that study. In the meantime, I'm doing my part to make an already fantastic, centuries-old candy even better. Because who wants just three flavors anymore? Have your key lime pie and toffee, too, I say.

When I'm not driving my husband crazy with explosive kitchen experiments, I teach high school. That should tell you something about my grit, determination, and problem-solving abilities, all of which I've applied to candymaking. I'm excited for you to try the results!
