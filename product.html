Click the name to read the descriptions and ingredients for the toffees. Then, inquire about acquiring some, because damn it, you deserve it. I make each and every one by hand and invent all the flavors in the dead of night.

  • Potato Chip

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, salt, potato chips, 60% dark chocolate, soy lecithin

    Description

    Potato Chip toffee strikes me as the perfect snack. An addictive combo of salty and sweet, this toffee is also paper-thin, which makes it extra crunchable.

  • Coffee Toffee

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, molasses, ground espresso, salt, pecans/walnuts, 60% dark chocolate, soy lecithin

    Description

    This toffee goes very well with its namesake. With espresso-laced toffee and a dark chocolate coating, this is a more sophisticated, less sweet, flavor combination.

  • Key Lime Pie

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, Key Lime extract, salt, white chocolate, graham cracker, soy lecithin

    Description

    Key Lime Pie is one of those toffees that makes you do a double-take. It's thin and crunchy, yet it tastes just like the real thing: tart, sweet, and tropical. Features a white-choclate layer.

  • Original (no nuts)

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, salt, 60% dark chocolate, soy lecithin

    Description

    My original toffee is the one that got me started in this crazy enterprise. It's thin but has deep, complex flavors that were unique enough to want to share with others.

  • Original

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, salt, pecans, 60% dark chocolate, soy lecithin

    Description

    Like the original, it's salty, sweet, brittle, and not the holiday gift you'll want to stash in your fridge and forget until next year.

  • Snickerdoodle

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, molasses, cinnamon, snickerdoodle cookie crumbs (butter, sugar, flour, vanilla, cream of tartar, cinnamon, salt), salt, white chocolate, soy lecithin

    Description

    Snickerdoodle is fun, a little spicy, and very sweet. I generously sprinkly the white chocolate layer with Snickerdoodle crumbs, which makes this treat a unique cross between crispy toffee and cookie.

  • Pumpkin Pie

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, brown sugar, molasses, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, salt, pecans, pumpkin seeds, white chocolate, graham cracker, soy lecithin

    Description

    Pumpkin Pie is great year-round, but is perfect as a unique offering around the holidays. It's spicy, and very close to the taste of actual pumpkin pie.

  • Trail Mix

    Ingredients

    Description

    This is the snack you'll wish you had instead of that summer sausage when you hit the trail. Addictive toffee infused with all of the best parts of being in the wilderness, it's about five treats in one.

  • Birthday Cake

    Ingredients

    butter, white sugar, butter extract, salt, white chocolate, sprinkles, soy lecithin

    Description

    Birthday Cake is ultra-sweet and a total throwback to childhood. I worked on the recipe to evoke the flavors of vanilla cake and icing. With rainbow sprinkles and white chocolate, it's a celebration in a crunchy bite.
